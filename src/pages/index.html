Who

We are remote working web professionals specialising in responsive design and development. We can make you a simple WordPress backend or a custom CMS with an online store. We have been making websites for some time now and we’ve enjoyed the journey the internet has taken from desktops to laptops to smart phones. The internet is an exciting place and we love helping businesses get a web presence or refresh and rethink their current website.

Why

We have the experience and attention to detail to deliver work that elevates your brand and web presence. Coming from backgrounds in Advertising, Print tand Web Design and Web Development we can create full content managed websites, responsive HTML emails, HTML5 banners, stationery, and everything in between. We are small enough to keep our rates are very competitive and we can handle your job without the extra overheads of an agency.

Using current best practice streamlined workflows, we work in an agile fashion and have a short turnaround time. Technologies we use are Craft CMS, Wordpress, Foundation 5/6, Sass, Grunt, Gulp, NPM and many others. Our process is one of constant discovery and iterative improvement. We make our sites to gracefully degrade for older browsers and take advantage of features only offered by new browsers built to be “mobile first”.

Work

www.skybojapan.com

Skybo Japan needed a bi-lingual website quickly. They also wanted a refresh of their corporate ID. We designed a new logo and colour scheme to be used in a responsive website.
